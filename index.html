Welcome to my page

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Robotics Research Group at KU Leuven since January 2019. My research interests lie in the areas of artificial intelligence, automated planning, robotics, and robot learning. I completed my Ph.D. in AI Planning and Robotics at the University of Aveiro, Portugal. My doctoral dissertation contributes to the development of novel algorithms for knowledge representation and robot learning and task planning. My current research focuses on filling the gap between AI and robotics by the integration of AI planning into real robotic systems. My research has resulted in the development of some state-of-the-art techniques and open source tools in order for robot programming and planning.
